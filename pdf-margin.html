Pro Tips for Better PDF Margins

Choose the Right Preset

Use the Binding preset for documents that will be bound, or Symmetric for double-sided printing to ensure consistent margins on both sides.

Check Printer Settings

Ensure your margins match your printer's minimum printable area. Most printers need at least 5mm on each side.

Preview Before Printing

Always preview your document before printing to verify margins are correct and content isn't cut off.
