Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a legally binding signature?

This tool creates a digital signature for convenience purposes. For legally binding electronic signatures, you may need to use a certified e-signature service that complies with regulations like eIDAS or ESIGN Act.

Is there a file size limit?

Yes, the maximum file size is 50MB. This ensures the tool works efficiently in your browser without performance issues.

Can I sign multiple pages?

Currently, this tool adds the signature to a single page. You can specify which page to sign or leave it blank to sign the last page.

Can I customize the signature appearance?

Yes, you can choose between drawing or typing your signature, select different colors, and choose from several font options for text signatures.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely! All processing happens locally in your browser. Your files never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.

Can I use this tool on mobile devices?

Yes! Our PDF signer works perfectly on iPhone and Android devices. Just open your browser and start signing - no app installation required.

Which browsers are supported?

Our tool works on all modern browsers: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and Opera. For best performance, we recommend using the latest version.

Can I sign password-protected PDFs?

Currently, we don't support password-protected PDFs. Please remove the password before uploading.

How to sign PDF on Mac for free?

Easy! Open PDFMaster in Safari or Chrome on your Mac, upload your PDF, and sign it for free. No software to install, no watermarks added.

Why choose PDFMaster for signing PDFs?

PDFMaster offers 100% free PDF signing with no watermarks, local processing for complete privacy, and multiple signature options. Your files stay secure on your device.
