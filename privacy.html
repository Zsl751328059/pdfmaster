Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 24, 2026

Your Privacy Matters

At PDFMaster, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data. Unlike many online tools, we prioritize your privacy by processing all files locally in your browser.

PRIVACY GUARANTEE: Your files never leave your device. All PDF processing happens locally in your browser, ensuring complete confidentiality.

Information Collection

This website is an online tool service platform that does not actively collect users' personal private information.

Content uploaded, entered, or generated by users during tool usage is processed only within the current browser and is not uploaded to or stored on servers.

Log Information

To optimize service quality, the website may record access logs including visit time, browser type, device information, and other anonymous data, which does not contain personal identity information.

Information Usage

Collected anonymous data is used solely for service optimization, troubleshooting, and security protection, and will not be used for other purposes.

Information Sharing

This website will not sell, rent, or share any user information with third parties, unless required by law or to protect platform security.

Data Security

This website employs security technologies to protect user data. All tool processing is completed locally without uploading private content.

User Rights

Users may stop using this website at any time without concern about information being collected or leaked.

Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated periodically. Updates will be posted on this page, and continued use of this website constitutes agreement to the updated policy.
