Local Processing - Your Privacy, Protected

What does "local processing" mean? When you use our PDF Splitter, all file processing happens directly in your web browser, right on your device. Your files never leave your computer or mobile device - they are not uploaded to any external server.

This means your sensitive documents stay private and secure. There's no risk of data breaches, no third-party access, and no server logs containing your information. Even if you're offline, our tool continues to work because everything runs locally in your browser.

How It Works - Browser-Based Processing

⚙️

WebAssembly Power

We use WebAssembly (Wasm) to run PDF processing algorithms directly in your browser. This technology allows near-native performance while keeping everything client-side.

📚

PDF.js Integration

Our tool leverages Mozilla's PDF.js library for reliable PDF parsing and rendering. All operations happen locally - no server roundtrips, no data exposure.

🧠

Memory-Safe Architecture

We've implemented smart memory management to prevent browser slowdowns. Temporary objects are cleaned up immediately after use, ensuring smooth performance even with large files.
