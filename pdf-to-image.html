Always preview the converted images to ensure colors and layout are correct before downloading all pages.

Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats can I convert to?

You can convert PDF pages to either PNG (with transparent background) or JPEG (compressed format). PNG is best for documents with transparency or sharp graphics, while JPEG is better for photos and offers smaller file sizes.

Is there a file size limit?

Yes, the maximum file size is 50MB. This ensures the tool works efficiently in your browser without performance issues.

Can I convert specific pages only?

Yes, you can select which pages to convert by clicking on the page thumbnails in the options tab. This allows you to extract only the pages you need.

What resolution options are available?

We offer three resolution options: 72 DPI (screen quality), 150 DPI (medium quality), and 300 DPI (high quality for printing). Higher resolution produces larger image files.

How do I convert PDF without losing quality?

For best quality results, select the 300 DPI resolution and PNG format. This preserves sharp details and maintains the original document quality.

Is this tool completely free?

Yes, our converter is 100% free to use with no hidden fees, no watermarks, and no registration required.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely! All processing happens locally in your browser. Your files never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.

Can I process multiple PDFs at once?

Yes, you can upload and convert multiple PDF files simultaneously for efficient batch processing.

Does it work with scanned PDFs?

Yes, our tool handles both text-based and scanned PDFs. Scanned documents are converted as image files.
