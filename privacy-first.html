Privacy First

Secure Browser-Based Processing

At PDFMaster, we believe your documents belong to you. That's why all PDF processing happens directly in your web browser. Unlike other online tools that upload your files to remote servers, we process everything locally on your device.

This means sensitive information never leaves your computer, ensuring complete privacy and security for your confidential documents. Whether you're working with legal contracts, financial reports, or personal documents, you can trust that your data remains private.

🔒 No data is ever uploaded to our servers

GDPR Compliant

Our privacy-first approach ensures GDPR compliance. Since we don't store or process your data on our servers, we comply with the strictest data protection regulations. You retain full control over your documents at all times.

GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) requires organizations to protect personal data and respect user privacy. By processing everything locally, we eliminate the need for data transfer, making our service inherently compliant with these regulations.
