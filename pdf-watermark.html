Pro Tips for Better PDF Watermarking

Choose Watermark Color Wisely

Select a color that's visible but not intrusive. Light gray works well for most documents.

Adjust Opacity Carefully

Too opaque and the watermark becomes distracting. Too transparent and it's ineffective.

Preview Before Applying

Always preview the watermark on a sample page to ensure it looks right.
