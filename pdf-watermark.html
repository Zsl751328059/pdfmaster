Pro Tips for Better PDF Watermarking

Choose Color Wisely

Select a color that's visible but not intrusive. Light gray works well for most documents. Test on a sample page first.

Adjust Opacity Carefully

Too opaque and the watermark becomes distracting. Too transparent and it's ineffective. 30-50% is usually optimal.

Preview Before Applying

Always preview the watermark on multiple pages before downloading. This ensures consistent appearance throughout.
