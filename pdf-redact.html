Pro Tips for Better PDF Redaction

Be Thorough

Check every page carefully for sensitive information. Don't forget headers, footers, watermarks, and metadata that might contain information you want to hide.

Use Appropriate Box Sizes

Make sure your redaction boxes fully cover the sensitive content, including any partial characters at the edges. Better slightly too large than too small.

Review Before Downloading

After applying redactions, preview the result to ensure all sensitive information is properly hidden before downloading the final document.
