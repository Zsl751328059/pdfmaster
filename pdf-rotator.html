FAQ

Can I rotate specific pages only?

Yes, you can rotate PDF pages selectively by clicking on individual pages in the preview. If no pages are selected, all pages will be rotated by default.

Is there a file size limit?

Yes, the maximum file size is 50MB. This ensures the PDF rotator works efficiently in your browser without performance issues.

What rotation angles are supported?

We support 90°, 180°, and 270° rotation. Rotate PDF 90 degrees clockwise, 180 degrees to flip upside down, or 270 degrees counter-clockwise.

Will rotating affect the file quality?

No, rotating PDF pages is a lossless operation. You can rotate PDF without losing quality - the content remains unchanged.
