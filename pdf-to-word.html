How PDF to Word Conversion Works

PDF.js Parsing

We use Mozilla's PDF.js library to parse PDF content directly in your browser. Text extraction and formatting are handled locally without server dependency.

DOCX Generation

Extracted content is converted to structured DOCX format using docx.js library. This preserves paragraphs and basic formatting for easy editing.

Memory-Safe Architecture

We've implemented smart memory management to prevent browser slowdowns. Temporary objects are cleaned up immediately after use.

Pro Tips for Better PDF to Word Conversion

Check PDF Quality

Ensure your PDF contains selectable text. Scanned image-based PDFs require OCR conversion first for best results.

Review Formatting

Complex layouts may need manual adjustment after conversion. Tables and multi-column text may require reformatting in Word.

Preserve Original

Always keep your original PDF as a backup. Conversion may not perfectly preserve all formatting in complex documents.
